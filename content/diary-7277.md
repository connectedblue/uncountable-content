<p class="wp-block-paragraph">The annual summer walk today with <a href="https://www.cotswolds-nl.org.uk/looking-after/our-grasslands-projects/glorious-cotswolds-grasslands/">Glorious Grasslands</a> where we visit a site where we did winter habitat management to see how it looks in full bloom.  This year was a beautiful site near Cleeve Hill that we&#8217;ve done many times and most recently <a href="https://diary.uncountable.uk/2026/01/finishing-off-the-cleeve-hill-quarry/" data-type="post" data-id="6868">earlier this year</a>.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">On first view of the grassland, we knew we&#8217;d be in for a treat.  Loads of striking Viper&#8217;s Blugloss dotted up the steep banks.  Everywhere we looked was packed with plants.  Just one small bank contained pretty much all the indicator species you could imagine.</p>

<p class="wp-block-paragraph">We stopped for coffee and cake break at the top before making our way over to the far field where Chris and I did a lot of brush cutting in deep dips were covered in bramble.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">It was very pleasing to see grass now instead and only a small bit of bramble grown back.  There was also quite a lot of tall St John&#8217;s Wort which apparently thrives on ground that has been disturbed.</p>

<p class="wp-block-paragraph">There was one 100 sq meter section of this seeding that was brush cut very low by Tom before applying the seed.  We don&#8217;t normally do this, but it was an experiment in case the rest of the field didn&#8217;t take.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">It was astonishing to see this area very clearly &#8211; a pretty much perfect rectangle packed with all our classic species.</p>
</div></div>

<p class="wp-block-paragraph">As it turned out, the restoration was a big success anyway, but this little section will act as a seed bank nursery for many decades to come.  After a quick picnic lunch, we managed to get away before the rain came in.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">In the evening we had the regular <a href="https://dursleygreen.org.uk/dursley-green-drinks/">Dursley Green Drinks</a>, which was pretty well attended with a couple of new people as well.</p>
