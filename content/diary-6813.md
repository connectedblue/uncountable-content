<p>This was the final bit of a huge section of scrub I&#8217;ve been clearing from there over the last few months.  We now have a very large area that can turn into grassland during the next growing season.</p>



<p>I was also amazed by the cattle grazing that took place over the last month. <a href="https://diary.uncountable.uk/2025/12/prepping-for-orchard-hedge-laying/" data-type="post" data-id="6737">Last time I was here</a> in December we opened the gates to let the cattle in to the long grass, and today the entire orchard grassland was grazed very short.</p>



<p>In the afternoon, i had a call with <a href="https://www.gcer.co.uk/">GCER</a> and <a href="https://www.cotswolds-nl.org.uk/looking-after/our-grasslands-projects/glorious-cotswolds-grasslands/">Glorious Grasslands</a> to discuss how we can upload bulk records from this year&#8217;s botanical surveys into the official Gloucestershire records.  I have about 14,000 records and it will be a pretty straightforward task to prepare them for upload.  Hopefully I&#8217;ll have this done by the end of March.</p>
