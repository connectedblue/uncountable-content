I was especially interested to see this site after hearing the farm manager Jake give <a href="https://diary.uncountable.uk/2023/11/regenerative-agriculture-and-a-new-car/" data-type="post" data-id="2652">a talk in Wotton</a> a couple of years ago.</p>

<p>The task today was to remove a large number of hawthorn saplings from a grassland to allow sheep grazing.  It looked quite daunting at first, but we split into three teams:  popping, lopping and Chris and I on the brush cutters.  </p>



<p>We managed good progress with the brush cutter, able to go through all but the thickest of stems.  We then tossed the arisings into large piles which the farm JCB and tractor could scoop up and remove.</p>



<p>Jake laid on some excellent soup and rolls at lunchtime which went down very well.  After about another hour or so of cutting and clearing, most of the field was done &#8211; a huge improvement on what we encountered in the morning.  </p>
