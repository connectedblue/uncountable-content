<p>I&#8217;ve been ill.  For an unexpectedly long time.  I caught a flu virus and have been knocked out for over a week. </p>



<p>I felt somewhat better this morning and was determined to get out and walk for an hour.  Nothing too strenuous, just the pathways around the village.  The wild garlic and primroses were out, and it felt good to be out in the open again.</p>



<p>I&#8217;m hoping to get out tomorrow for a slightly longer walk, with a view to starting activities again next week.  </p>
