<p>There were a huge number of runners wearing fancy dress, and five races in all, including a children&#8217;s race and wheelchair race.  This was a community event at it&#8217;s best &#8211; quirky, inclusive and fun.  </p>



<p>After the event, Georgie and I went to the Old Spot to do some planning for the forthcoming <a href="https://offbeatcollective.org.uk/">Offbeat Dursley</a> event in the summer.  We went through everything and sketched a programme that is both do-able with the time remaining and true to the creative spirit of the experience.</p>
