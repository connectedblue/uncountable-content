<p>First winter habitat management of the season for me with <a href="https://www.cotswolds-nl.org.uk/looking-after/our-grasslands-projects/glorious-cotswolds-grasslands/">Glorious Grasslands</a>, and returned to the site we <a href="https://diary.uncountable.uk/2025/03/final-resthaven/" data-type="post" data-id="4489">finished last season</a> at.  The task at Resthaven is to open up the grassland at the back where grazing can be enabled later in the season.</p>



<p>The weather was forecast to be rainy and didn&#8217;t disappoint.  The first task for me was to clear up raked grass from what the team did last week with the power scythe.  I then went and helped Jane and Ruth with the invasive buddleia growing by the woodland edge.  This took us up to break time where they laid on coffee and biscuits for us.</p>

<p>We seeded the area to try and encourage grass and wildflowers to out compete the nettles and thistles next season.  Finally we went down to the woodland boundary to clear branches which had fallen into the neighbouring field when cut.</p>



<p>When I returned home, I found an email which meant we had to cancel the scheduled <a href="https://dursleygreen.org.uk/dursley-green-drinks/">Dursley Green Drinks</a> this evening which was rather unfortunate.  Jo and I managed to get communications out on our channels in time, but we still went down anyway in case anyone didn&#8217;t receive the message.  Just as well because one person did turn up, so at least we were able to have a small chat with them.</p>
