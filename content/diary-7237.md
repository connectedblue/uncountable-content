<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Met up with Graham first thing for our regular monthly riverfly check at the ocean.  The weather has suddenly turned &#8211; only around 11 degrees and needed coats and fleeces.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">The flow and depth were fine, and the initial sample bucket looked pretty promising &#8211; even seeing a mayfly which we&#8217;ve not seen for a few weeks at other sites.  There were both a wide number of species as well as good counts for a couple of them.  Overall the score was 9 which we were pretty happy with.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">In the afternoon, I headed over to the library for the second <a href="https://www.facebook.com/dursleycodeclub">Dursley Code Club</a> of the week.  A full house today with all eight stations taken, and even a five year old showing some interest on one of the laptops.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">The sound issues on the new PCs had been fixed, and I tested the python install on the browser which also worked, so that makes some of the tasks a bit easier for the club.</p>
