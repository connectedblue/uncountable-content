<p>The annual Club and Society fair was held at the <a href="https://www.chantrycentre.org/">Chantry Centre</a> in the morning, so I took the opportunity to go down and meet with any local organisations who are not yet on <a href="https://discoverdursley.org.uk/">Discover Dursley</a>.</p>



<p>It&#8217;s a very well attended event, and was a good opportunity to learn about lots of organisations I hadn&#8217;t come across before, and I left with a stack of leaflets to follow up on entries for the website.</p>
