It&#8217;s a large site, and I spent most of the day at the far end where Butterfly Conservation carry out surveys of the Wall Brown butterfly.</p>

<p>It was quite a tangle of hawthorn scrub and bramble.  I didn&#8217;t want to just slice them all because John had a tree popper and it&#8217;s much more beneficial if we can pull out the root as well.</p>



<p>Although the rest of the country was experiencing a severe storm, we had pretty glorious weather, especially in the morning.  By lunchtime we had all the scrub cut from most of the wall and the quarry dips, so we were joined by the rest of the team to help rake down and remove the nutrient ready for the pony grazing later in the year.</p>



<p>We&#8217;ll be returning to this site in June for our summer walk and picnic, so looking forward to seeing the results of the habitat management.</p>
