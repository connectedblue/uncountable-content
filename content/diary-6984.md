<p>The depth was not too bad, but the flow and visibility in the centre of the river was not safe to sample.  So we stayed near the bank and kicked the sample for three minutes.</p>



<p>First glance, an abundance of mayfly was clear. Certainly more than I&#8217;ve ever seen at this particular site, and possibly at any site.</p>



<p>There were also a healthy number of cased caddis, so a score of 4 from these two species alone.</p>



<p>But no olives or any other riverfly which was a little odd.</p>
