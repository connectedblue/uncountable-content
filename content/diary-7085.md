We were working by the entrance at the A38, where a rough landscaped area needed grass seeding and some posts needed to be installed for a willow screen next to the canal.</p>

<p>The posts were 2.4m long, so 160cm would stay above ground and the remaining third buried.  The wrecking bar was useful to make the starter hole, but the ground was pretty soft.</p>



<p>The width was pretty crucial because the willow panels will be premade and 183cm wide.  There will be ten in all, so quite a few posts to bash in.</p>



<p>As ever with these tasks, a lot of time was spent planning what we would do, so only one was in by morning break.  But we then managed another four before lunch and one after, so pretty good progress.  Should only be one more session to complete the posts and it&#8217;ll only be quick to attach the panels themselves.</p>
