<p class="wp-block-paragraph">I bumped into Martin a couple of weeks ago at Fromebridge, and he suggested we catch up on various things going on. He also wanted some guidance on what do to about his website renewal.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">So since it&#8217;s been a while since I&#8217;ve been down at Wild Acres, I suggested we meet this morning down there.  It was good to see the progress &#8211; some things are the same and other things have moved on.  Overall the food production is in good shape and the meadow was looking great, even though he didn&#8217;t manage to get a cut in before the growing season.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">We made a pot of tea and sat for around 2.5 hours in the summer house talking about pretty much everything going on around the local community.  There&#8217;s a couple of potential projects where we might have a common interest in pursuing, so we&#8217;ll chew on that and see if there&#8217;s any capacity to follow through.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">All in all, a pleasant morning and just nice to spend time without having to do anything in particular.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">In the afternoon, it was off to the library again for the regular <a href="https://www.facebook.com/dursleycodeclub">Dursley Code Club</a>.  Some interesting projects using ollama and the raspberry pi, and also a super creative application to track homework assignments.</p>
