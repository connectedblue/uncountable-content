I&#8217;ve been on quite a few tree planting sessions with SVP over the years, but surprisingly never this site before.</p>

<p>Without this annual work, the area would just bramble over.  Although rough grassland today, the plan over the coming decades is for this to become a substantial woodland.  There&#8217;s a nice mix of native trees, which will provide a really diverse habitat.</p>



<p>In the afternoon I met a builder for an initial chat about quoting for some major work on the house.  He had some very good practical observations about the current design which I&#8217;ll have to take back to the architect for an alternative option.</p>
