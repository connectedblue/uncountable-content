<p>Rather bizarrely, I came across a buried mink raft right at the back.  Given that this has not been accessible in the last five or six years we&#8217;ve been here, I&#8217;d estimate it&#8217;s been there at least 10-15 years ago.  But is interesting evidence that this corner mustn&#8217;t have been so overgrown relatively recently.</p>



<p>It took almost all the session to clear this patch and rake into a pile ready for the next bonfire.  The last part was a tangle of an old wood habitat pile and brambles.  This will need to be cut by hand in a future work party and added to the bonfire.</p>



<p>The area nearby was also covered in <a href="https://diary.uncountable.uk/2024/12/clearing-the-orchard-bank/" data-type="post" data-id="3955">head high bramble last year</a> which I had to clear to make way for fencing.  The encouraging thing is this is now covered in grassland, so it recovered very quickly.  If we can rake this area off in a similar way, it should also provide an excellent corner of new meadow grasses.</p>
