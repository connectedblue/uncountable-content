<p>Once this was done, I waited for two former members of Code Club to join me after school.  I&#8217;ve got a forthcoming project for them involving a digital puzzle for the new <a href="https://www.theengine.org.uk/the-escape-room-dursley">Dursley Escape Room</a> in the new year.</p>



<p>So we spent a good hour and a half going through the requirements.  They had some excellent ideas and suggestions, so I really think this will be an exciting activity to develop with them over the next few months.</p>
