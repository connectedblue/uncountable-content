<p>There were six of us again on the hedge, while the rest of the team went to plant a hedge in the small meadow on the other side of the reserve.</p>



<p>We split into three teams.  The first continued laying the next multi stem tree in the line from last week.  The second started preparing the cuts on stems further back, but not laying yet.</p>



<p>And the rest of us went down the each side, clearing thick bramble and tangled side branches to reveal the stems for laying.</p>

<p>It worked pretty well.  We could place the brash on the floor between the upright stems before laying them down (more efficient that placing it on top which we did last week).  </p>



<p>Once the forward team had completed the large multi stem hawthorn, the remaining trees were like dominos.  The final result looked very good.  We still need to finish off with binding, which we&#8217;ll do in coming weeks.</p>
