<p>The route up contains quite a treacherous river crossing at Shilley Pool which was impassable at this time of year. So we backtracked to the road and reclimbed the other side to get out on the moor.</p>



<p>The visibility was good, but on reaching the summit it was high wind and light rain.  </p>

<p>Luckily the descent was quick to a lower point a lot more sheltered for lunch.  From this spot you could see quite a wide vista of devon countryside which seemed largely dedicated to pasture.</p>



<p>From here it was a pleasant descent through a nice nature reserve into the settlement of South Zeal and then an easy walk back through the lanes back to Throwleigh.</p>
