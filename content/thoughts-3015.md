<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Today is  <a href="https://www.volunteeramnestyday.net/">Volunteer Responsibility Amnesty Day</a>. It occurs twice a year on the longest and shortest day and is a chance for volunteers to reflect on the list of things they do, and decide if some changes need to be made to prevent them burning out.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">I volunteer with several organisations and within the community. It&#8217;s a really important way to get <a href="https://thoughts.uncountable.uk/replacing-work-benefits/" data-type="post" data-id="413">structure, identity and purpose</a> after you stop work. But it can get overwhelming. There are just so many interesting things to get involved with, it&#8217;s easy to over commit.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Since finding out about this day in 2024 I&#8217;ve found it a great way to constantly review what I do and not to be afraid to drop something if necessary. This is my <a href="/topic/volunteer-responsibility-amnesty-day/">fifth review</a>.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">There have been quite a few changes <a href="https://thoughts.uncountable.uk/winter-solstice-2025-volunteer-responsibility-amnesty-day/" data-type="post" data-id="2711">since my last update</a>. I stepped down from the admin team of Stroud Wildlife Survey team, and also Uley Trees and Meadows.  While these were not huge time consumers, they still took up head space and admin time which detract from other projects I organise.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Having this twice yearly check point on my activities actually forces me to be much more intentional about pruning things (I still can&#8217;t seem to stop adding things, but the rate at which I add them is decreasing).</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">The main things I added were Digital Dursley on the organising front.  This is a new group that has been simmering for a while, but if we are to do something with it, it will require some significant allocation of time on my part. </p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">I also joined the Box Wood work party which meets every couple of weeks.  This is low commitment, and box wood is a place I like to be so that fits well. </p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Overall, I feel I&#8217;ve done more community projects in the first half of 2026 that conservation ones.  I spent a huge number of hours on an app for the Local Escape room.  I also spent a large amount of time refactoring the Discover Dursley code as a bespoke plugin which will make maintenance much easier in the future.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">So, here is my current list of volunteering activities that reflect the above and other changes.</p>

Having this amnesty day in the calendar has really helped me keep control and perspective on my volunteering activities.</p>
