<p>Seven volunteers from the <a href="https://www.cucan.org.uk/Uley-trees-and-meadows">Uley Trees &amp; Meadows</a> group met in the morning by the bench on the Millennium Green to do some habitat management before the growing season starts.</p>



<p>Under the direction of Richard, we set to work on cutting and raking the grass, removing shoots from stumps of previously cut scrub and knocking back the blackthorn.</p>



<p>This is the third year we&#8217;ve managed this particular slope and you can see the difference in the plant variety coming through even at this time of year.  Last year Richard counted 10 different species of butterfly when sitting on the bench.  There is also an elusive dipper in the area.</p>



<p>Definitely will be worth visiting again from the end of May to see the abundance of wildflowers in full bloom.  </p>
