<p>In the centre of the grassland are three very large bramble patches.  The plan is to reduce them slowly over time and allow the grassland to take over.  So our instruction was to reduce one of them by around 80%.</p>



<p>By lunch break we managed to complete that task, and the team were able to then thoroughly rake around.  I then moved further up the grassland to a steep bank section that couldn&#8217;t be done with the power mower.  It turned out to be pretty difficult to navigate.  Footing was not good underneath and the gradient was so steep that often the brush cutter blade would just hover over the grass.</p>



<p>Good turnout from the local volunteers and managed to get everything cut and mostly raked by 2.30pm.</p>
