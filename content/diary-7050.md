<p>I floated the idea of doing a riverfly taster session at one of the sites on the Frome.  This is something we can do any time of year, so I&#8217;ll try and get something organised for late summer.</p>



<p>We broke for a buffet lunch and chat, and then an excellent talk from Graeme on Wildlife gardening.  Graeme&#8217;s talks are always valuable and packed with information and today was no exception.  <br><br>Quite worrying is the rapid decline in invertebrate numbers in the last couple of years.  While I&#8217;m aware of the general picture over several decades, I hadn&#8217;t picked up that things had got that bad recently.  So this just underscores the importance of wildlife gardening.</p>
